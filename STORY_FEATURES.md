# Story Features Roadmap

## Editing & Management
- [ ] Edit Story Caption - Basic necessity
- [ ] Archive Stories - Save stories before they expire
- [ ] Story Drafts - Save incomplete stories to post later (future)
- [ ] Scheduled Stories - Post stories at a specific time (future)

## Engagement Features
- [ ] Story Polls - Add interactive polls to stories
- [ ] Story Questions - Q&A stickers for followers
- [ ] Story Quizzes - Multiple choice questions
- [ ] Story Countdowns - Countdown timer to events
- [ ] Story Music - Add background music/audio
- [ ] Story Links - Swipe-up links (premium feature?)
- [ ] Story Highlights - Save stories permanently to profile
- [ ] Story Replies - Direct message responses to stories
- [ ] Story Forwarding - Share someone's story to your story

## Creative Tools
- [ ] Filters & Effects - Photo/video filters
- [ ] Text Overlays - Custom text with fonts/colors
- [ ] Stickers & GIFs - Add stickers, emojis, GIFs
- [ ] Drawing Tools - Draw/doodle on stories
- [ ] Boomerang/Loop - Create looping videos
- [ ] Collage Mode - Multiple photos in one story
- [ ] Story Backgrounds - Colored backgrounds for text stories

## Privacy & Control
- [ ] Hide Story From Users - Block specific users from seeing
- [ ] Close Friends List - Share stories only with close friends
- [ ] Story Download Prevention - Disable downloads
- [ ] Mute Stories - Hide specific users' stories from feed

## Analytics & Insights
- [ ] Story Analytics - Views, completion rate, engagement
- [ ] Viewer Demographics - Who viewed (age, location if available)
- [ ] Peak Viewing Times - When most people view
- [ ] Story Reach - How far your story traveled
- [ ] Engagement Rate - Reactions/views ratio

## Discovery & Social
- [ ] Story Search - Search stories by hashtags/location
- [ ] Trending Stories - Most viewed/engaged stories
- [ ] Story Categories - Tag stories (food, travel, etc.)
- [ ] Story Hashtags - Add hashtags for discovery
- [ ] Story Challenges - Participate in viral challenges
- [ ] Collaborative Stories - Multiple users contribute to one story

## Notifications & Reminders
- [ ] Story Reminders - Remind to post daily
- [ ] Viewer Notifications - Alert when specific people view
- [ ] Expiry Warnings - Notify before story expires
- [ ] Engagement Notifications - Alert on reactions/shares

## Technical Enhancements
- [ ] Story Compression - Optimize media size
- [ ] Auto-Quality - Adjust quality based on connection
- [ ] Story Preloading - Preload next stories for smooth viewing
- [ ] Offline Viewing - Cache stories for offline access
